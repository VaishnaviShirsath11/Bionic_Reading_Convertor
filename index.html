What is Bionic Reading?

Bionic reading is a reading technique that emphasizes some letters of words to help readers process words faster and more efficiently. It uses artificial fixation points to guide the eyes through text by selectively bolding parts of words, such as the first half of each word. The brain processes words faster than the eyes can read individual letters, so focusing on fewer letters at a time helps readers process faster while still understanding the full meaning. Bionic reading can be beneficial for people with ADHD or dyslexia, or anyone who wants to read more smoothly.
